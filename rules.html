Frequently Asked Questions

Can I participate individually?

While we encourage team participation (2–5 members), individual participants are also welcome to join.

What are the submission requirements?

Teams must submit a working prototype/demo, presentation deck, and documentation explaining the project's impact and technical approach.

Is there a registration fee?

No, participation in Taqathon is completely free for all registered participants.

What resources will be provided?

Participants will have access to mentors, APIs, datasets, cloud credits, and development tools throughout the hackathon.
